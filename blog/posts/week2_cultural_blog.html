Exploring My Values

In this post I will take some times to explore the values that are most important to me.

Values I associate with being the happiest

The values that I most associate with happiness or satisfaction are the following:

  • Competition
  • Comfort
  • Predictability
  • Having a family

In depth about one of these values

Predictability is very important to me because I like to know what to expect. When I am in a new situation I often feel uncomfortable. Also, I have a hard time when someone else does something that surprises me. I guess in general I don't like surprises. This all seems kind of negative when I write it, but I'm happiest when everything is expected and as it should be.

Do I live up to these values?

Last topic someone asked my advice on

Recently one of my employees was really bummed because some of his students did not like him as a teacher. He was feeling like a real failure and that he let the students down. I asked him if he had done anything differently from before and if he felt what he was teaching was valuable. He felt that he taught as he always did.

Based on this, I felt that the issue may have been with his students rather than with his teaching. I don't like to dismiss a student's feedback, but it isn't always correct. This goes into something I try to follow, which is to remember that I can only control myself and what I do. If I do my best, then there isn't anything else I can do.
